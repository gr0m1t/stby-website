Real-time sync

WebSocket-based architecture keeps every device in perfect sync. State changes propagate in milliseconds across your entire network.

QLab integration

Full OSC/UDP support. Fire standby, go, and idle cues directly from QLab network cues using standard address patterns.

Assign channels to up to three colour-coded groups. Fire Group A, B, or C simultaneously with a single tap — perfect for complex productions.

Setup in minutes

How STBY works

01

Start the server

Open the STBY Mac app, select your show network interface, and click Start Server. Your URL appears instantly.

02

Connect your devices

Open the URL on any iPad or device. The mobile app finds the server automatically. Master view is PIN protected.

03

Set up your channels

Name your channels to match your positions — DSR, DSL, Flys, Spot 1. Changes sync to all connected devices immediately.

04

Fire cues

Tap STBY, GO, and CLR from the master iPad, the Mac app, or directly from QLab via OSC. Your talent sees it instantly.
